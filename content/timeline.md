## A Guardian Rises
- Location: cosmodrome
- Era: D1 · 2014

Centuries after the Collapse, a Ghost sparks life back into your bones amid the rusted hulls of the Cosmodrome. You are Risen — a Guardian of the Last City, with no memory of who you were.

- Quest: Restoration — Recover a working jumpship from the Fallen-held Cosmodrome and break atmosphere before the House of Devils overruns you.
- Gear: Khvostov 7G-02 — A battered Golden Age auto rifle, scavenged off the Cosmodrome floor. Your first weapon — and, one day, the foundation of a legend.
- Faction: House of Devils — Four-armed Fallen scavengers who have picked the old Wall clean for centuries. Your very first enemy.
- Triumph: First Light — Take your first breath as one of the Risen. The journey of a thousand worlds begins with a single resurrection.

### Where it begins
The Cosmodrome, in old Russia, was humanity's gateway to the stars during the Golden Age. Now its launch gantries are a rusted graveyard, picked over by the Fallen and haunted by the things that crawled in after the Collapse.

### Why it matters
- Your Ghost spent centuries searching the battlefield for the one corpse it could bring back — you.
- The Khvostov you scavenge here is the very first weapon of the saga, and it returns, reforged, at its very end.
- Everything that follows — every god you topple — starts with a single Guardian standing up in the dust.

## The Last City
- Location: tower
- Era: D1 · 2014

Your Ghost carries you to the Tower, humanity’s final bastion beneath the slumbering Traveler. Here Guardians gather, rearm, and trade tales between impossible battles.

- Quest: A New Light — Report to the Vanguard and take your first assignment in defense of the Last City.
- Gear: First Engram — Decrypt your first reward at the Cryptarch. Everything you carry, you earned.
- Faction: The Vanguard — The three commanders who guide the Guardians: a Titan, a Hunter, and a Warlock. Your first real allies.

### The Last Safe City
Built beneath the Traveler after the Collapse, the Last City is the final refuge of humanity. Its walls have held against the Fallen, the Cabal, and worse — sometimes only because Guardians threw themselves into the gap.

### Who you'll meet
- Commander Zavala — the Titan who holds the line.
- Ikora Rey — the Warlock who runs the Hidden.
- Cayde-6 — the Hunter who makes the Tower laugh, while it still can.

## The Dark Below
- Location: moon
- Era: D1 · 2014

On the silent Moon you breach the Hellmouth, a wound that drops for miles into the dark. Below it the Hive nest in their millions, and a god-prince named Crota waits to wake.

- Quest: The Sword of Crota — Descend into the Hellmouth and shatter the Hive’s blade before Crota stirs.
- Gear: Gjallarhorn — A rocket launcher of legend whose wolfpack rounds have ended more nightmares than any Guardian can count.
- Faction: The Hive — Ancient death-worshippers who feed on the end of worlds. Their power grows with every life they take.
- Triumph: Eyes Up, Guardian — Survive the Hellmouth and come back into the light. Few do.

## The Vault of Glass
- Location: venus
- Era: D1 · 2014

In the steaming jungles of Venus the Vex have folded time itself inside a mountain. You force open the Vault of Glass and unmake an enemy that was never quite real to begin with.

- Quest: Eye of a Gate Lord — Claim a Gate Lord’s eye to pierce the Vault’s defenses and step outside of time.
- Faction: The Vex — Time-bending machines that simulate the universe to predict and erase it. To them, you are a rounding error.
- Faction: House of Winter — The Fallen who claim the Ishtar Sink, ever at war with the Vex over Venus’s ruins.
- Triumph: Time's Vengeance — Conquer Atheon, Time’s Conflux, and prove that even a machine that has already won can still lose.

## Into the Black Garden
- Location: mars
- Era: D1 · 2014

The Cabal hold the red dunes of Mars, but deeper still lies the Black Garden — a place outside the system entirely. You strike down its Heart and return a fragment of the Traveler’s Light.

- Quest: The Black Garden — Find the garden that does not appear on any map, and slay the Heart of Darkness at its center.
- Gear: The Last Word — A hand cannon as fast as a guilty thought, carried by a gunslinger long dead. Yours, the moment you draw.
- Faction: The Cabal — A militant interstellar empire of armoured rhinos who take worlds by sheer industrial force.
- Triumph: A Light Returns — Restore the Traveler’s Light and become the Guardian the City has been waiting for.

## House of Wolves
- Location: reef
- Era: D1 · 2015

Summoned to the Reef, the Awoken queen Mara Sov turns you loose upon the House of Wolves — Fallen who swore her fealty, then broke it. Their leader Skolas would crown himself Kell of Kells.

- Quest: The Wolf Hunt — Run Skolas to ground across Venus, the Reef, and the ruins of Earth.
- Gear: Queenbreaker — An Awoken wire rifle that fires a lance of blinding void light.
- Faction: The Awoken — Children of the Reef, born of a catastrophe between Light and Dark, ruled by the enigmatic Mara Sov.
- Faction: House of Wolves — Oathbreaker Fallen who once served the Queen. Now they hunt a crown that was never theirs to take.

## The Taken King
- Location: dreadnaught
- Era: D1 · 2015

Oryx comes to avenge his son, dragging a continent-sized Dreadnaught into Saturn’s rings. His grief twists your fallen foes into the Taken. You board the warship to end the King of the Hive himself.

- Quest: Regicide — Walk the length of the Dreadnaught and cut Oryx down inside his own throne world.
- Gear: Touch of Malice — A scout rifle that feeds on the wielder’s own life to fire a final, ruinous round.
- Faction: The Taken — Souls ripped from reality and remade as Oryx’s puppets — blighted, blinking, wrong.
- Triumph: King's Fall — Topple the Taken King and carve your name into the deepest fear of the Hive.

## Rise of Iron
- Location: cosmodrome
- Era: D1 · 2016

A long-buried plague called SIVA erupts in the Plaguelands beyond the Wall. Lord Saladin, the last of the Iron Lords, calls you back to old Earth to take up a mantle the world thought lost.

- Quest: The Iron Lords — Walk the path of the fallen heroes and earn the right to wear their mark.
- Gear: Outbreak Prime — A pulse rifle that turns the SIVA plague back against itself in swarms of self-replicating nanites.
- Faction: The Splicers — Fallen of the House of Devils who have fused themselves with SIVA, the self-replicating nanotech that once doomed the Iron Lords.
- Triumph: Felwinter's Legacy — Become an Iron Lord, and carry the memory of those who fell so the City could stand.

## The Red War
- Location: edz
- Era: D2 · 2017

Ghaul’s Red Legion storms the Last City and cages the Traveler, tearing the Light from every Guardian at once. Powerless and nearly dead, you flee into the wilds of the European Dead Zone to begin again.

- Quest: Spark — Cross the EDZ to a shard of the Traveler and reclaim a sliver of the Light that was stolen.
- Gear: Sweet Business — A roaring minigun of an auto rifle that rewards you for never letting go of the trigger.
- Faction: The Red Legion — Ghaul’s elite Cabal warhost — the army that did what no one else ever could: it took your Light.
- Triumph: The Red War — Cast Ghaul down from the Traveler’s cage and give the City back its dawn.

## Savior of the Deep
- Location: titan
- Era: D2 · 2017

On Titan, Saturn’s drowned moon, methane seas churn above and the Hive fester in the flooded arcologies below. Commander Sloane holds a line that everyone else has already abandoned.

- Quest: Enemy of My Enemy — Hold the rigs of Titan and keep the abyss from swallowing what little remains.
- Gear: Tractor Cannon — A shotgun that throws enemies across the room like leaves in a storm.
- Faction: The Hive — Resurgent in the deep places of Titan, gnawing at the bones of a Golden Age that drowned long ago.

## The Disgraced AI
- Location: nessus
- Era: D2 · 2017

Nessus is a planetoid half-eaten and rebuilt by the Vex. The fractured AI Failsafe — equal parts chipper and despairing — guides you through the wreck of the Exodus ship she was born to fly.

- Quest: Looped — Descend the Inverted Spire and recover the Vex tech the Vanguard needs to fight back.
- Gear: Sunshot — A hand cannon that marks the dead and detonates them in chains of solar fire.
- Faction: The Vex — Converting Nessus molecule by molecule into a machine that thinks like they do.
- Triumph: Cradle of the Vex — Best the Inverted Spire and prove the machines do not own the future yet.

## The Last Touch
- Location: io
- Era: D2 · 2017

Io is the final world the Traveler blessed before the Collapse, and its soil still hums with that grace. Pilgrims, Vex, and Taken alike are all drawn to the last place a god showed mercy.

- Quest: Ghost Stories — Walk Io’s sacred sites and listen for what the Traveler left behind.
- Gear: Polaris Lance — A scout rifle whose precise final round blooms into solar fire.
- Faction: The Taken — Drawn to Io’s lingering Light like moths to the last candle in a dark house.

## Curse of Osiris
- Location: mercury
- Era: D2 · 2017

Within Mercury’s Infinite Forest, the Vex simulate a thousand thousand futures — and in every one, the Light is snuffed out. The exiled Warlock Osiris hunts the Mind Panoptes to break the cycle.

- Quest: The Gateway — Enter the Infinite Forest with Osiris and walk through futures that should never be.
- Gear: Prometheus Lens — A trace rifle that pours a continuous beam of focused solar flame.
- Faction: The Vex of the Forest — Simulated legions endlessly rehearsing your defeat until they can make it real.
- Triumph: Tree of Probabilities — Defy the simulation and prove that even a future the Vex have already calculated can still be rewritten.

## Warmind
- Location: mars
- Era: D2 · 2018

Mars’s polar caps melt and wake Rasputin, humanity’s ancient Warmind — and beneath the ice, the Hive worm-god Xol gnaws upward through the Hellas Basin toward the surface.

- Quest: Off-World — Reach Rasputin’s bunker and decide whether humanity’s oldest weapon is friend or threat.
- Gear: Sleeper Simulant — A linear fusion rifle forged from Golden Age war-tech that punches clean through anything.
- Faction: Cult of Xol — Hive zealots who worship a worm-god of dread and dig to free it from the Martian ice.
- Triumph: Spire of Stars — Strike the shadow of the Almighty and stop the sun from being turned against the City.

## Forsaken
- Location: dreamingcity
- Era: D2 · 2018

A prison break in the Reef ends with Cayde-6 dead at the hand of Prince Uldren Sov. Grief becomes a hunt — across the Tangled Shore, after the Barons, and into a cursed Awoken city that loops the same three weeks forever.

- Quest: The Machinist — Hunt the Barons one by one across the Tangled Shore and take back what they stole.
- Gear: Ace of Spades — Cayde-6’s own hand cannon. You carry it now, and you carry him.
- Faction: The Scorn — Risen, rotted Fallen freed from the Prison of Elders — neither alive nor truly dead.
- Triumph: Last Wish — Descend into the Dreaming City, outwit Riven — the last Ahamkara — and learn that some wishes can never be unmade.

## Shadowkeep
- Location: moon
- Era: D2 · 2019

You return to the Moon to find it changed — a red Hive temple risen from the dust, and Nightmares of the dead you could not save stalking the craters. Beneath it all a Pyramid waits, silent and patient. The Darkness has finally come.

- Quest: In Search of Answers — Breach the Scarlet Keep and confront the power stirring under the lunar surface.
- Gear: Divinity — A trace rifle forged from Vex remains that cages an enemy in a field of pure Light.
- Faction: Nightmares — Phantoms of your greatest losses and failures, given form and teeth by the Darkness beneath the Moon.
- Triumph: Shadowkeep — Stand before the first Pyramid and feel the Darkness, at long last, look back at you.

## Beyond Light
- Location: europa
- Era: D2 · 2020

On the frozen Jovian moon of Europa, the Fallen warlord Eramis wields Stasis — a shard of the Darkness itself. To stop her, you must take up that same forbidden power, and learn to walk the razor’s edge between Light and Dark.

- Quest: Born in Darkness — Master Stasis and claim a sliver of the Darkness’s gift as your own.
- Gear: Salvation's Grip — A Stasis grenade launcher of Clovis Bray design that freezes the battlefield solid.
- Faction: House of Salvation — Eramis’s Fallen, who would rather kneel to the Darkness than die in the cold.
- Triumph: Beyond Light — Wield the Darkness without letting it wield you. Few powers are so easily turned against their bearer.

## The Witch Queen
- Location: throneworld
- Era: D2 · 2022

Savathûn, the Witch Queen, has stolen the Light and built Ghosts of her own. In her swampy Throne World you face a Lucent Hive that resurrects exactly as you do — and a truth about the Light that no one ever wanted to learn.

- Quest: The Investigation — Unravel how Savathûn stole the Light, and what it means that she could.
- Gear: Osteo Striga — A poison SMG crafted at the Enclave — the first weapon you ever truly made with your own hands.
- Faction: Lucent Hive — Hive Guardians who wield the Light and rise from death again and again, just as you always have.
- Triumph: The Witch Queen — Out-think the cleverest liar in the universe inside the maze of her own mind.

## Lightfall
- Location: neptune
- Era: D2 · 2023

Calus, now a Disciple of the Witness, hunts the mysterious Veil in the hidden city of Neomuna on Neptune. Its Cloud Striders make a last stand while you grasp a new power — Strand — woven from the very fabric of reality.

- Quest: Headlong — Defend Neomuna and race the Witness to the secret of the Veil.
- Gear: Final Warning — A Strand sidearm that paints its targets and never misses what it has marked.
- Faction: Shadow Legion — Calus’s Darkness-touched Cabal, marching under a fallen emperor turned zealot.
- Triumph: Lightfall — Hold the line even as the Witness reaches the Traveler and the sky goes dark.

## The Final Shape
- Location: traveler
- Era: D2 · 2024

The Witness tears open the Traveler, and you follow it inside — into the Pale Heart, a dreamworld stitched from memory and possibility. Here, at the end of the Light and Darkness Saga, the war that began with your resurrection meets its final shape.

- Quest: Into the Light — Walk the Pale Heart, rally the Guardians, and carry the City’s hope into the Traveler itself.
- Gear: Still Hunt — A solar sniper rifle that holds the echo of Cayde’s Golden Gun in every shot.
- Faction: The Witness — The author of the Collapse and the architect of the Final Shape — the still, silent end of all things.
- Triumph: Salvation’s Edge — Reach the threshold of the Witness’s design and prepare to unmake it.

## Excision
- Location: traveler
- Era: D2 · 2024

Light and Dark as one, every Guardian who ever lived charges the Witness together. The saga that began with a single Ghost and a single forgotten corpse ends with all of them — united, unbroken, and unafraid.

- Gear: A Saga Ends — Ten years of war, ten years of worlds, ten years of resurrections — all of it, finished here.
- Faction: The Witness, Unveiled — No longer hidden, no longer patient. The final enemy stands revealed, and the whole of the Light answers.
- Triumph: The Final Shape — Stand shoulder to shoulder with every Guardian and unmake the Witness for good.

## Eternity
- Location: eternity
- Era: D2 · 2025

Beyond the saga’s end lies Eternity — a threshold where time and fate fold in on themselves. The great war is won, but something older and stranger stirs at the very edge of what the Light can still reach.

- Quest: Across the Threshold — Step beyond everything known and into Eternity, where the rules you trusted no longer hold.
- Gear: Echo of the Nine — A relic drawn from beyond the veil of fate, humming with a future not yet written.
- Faction: The Nine — Enigmatic beings born of the dark spaces between worlds, who deal in fate and probability.
- Triumph: Eternity — Cross into what comes after the end — and find that the story is not finished with you yet.

## The Lawless Frontier
- Location: frontier
- Era: D2 · 2025

Out past the order of the City lies the Lawless Frontier — uncharted, ungoverned, and crowded with those who would carve a new legend from the wreckage of the old war. A fresh age of Guardians begins here, on the ragged edge of everything.

- Quest: Stake Your Claim — Make your name in a place where the only authority is the one you build yourself.
- Gear: Frontier's Edge — A weapon forged for a lawless age, by hands that answer to no Vanguard.
- Faction: Renegades — Free agents, outlaws, and rogues staking their claims where no law from the Last City reaches.
- Triumph: The Lawless Frontier — Ride out into the next great unknown, and become a legend all over again.

## A Moment of Triumph
- Location: triumph
- Era: A DECADE OF LEGEND

From a nameless corpse in the Cosmodrome to the one who unmade the Witness and rode out past the edge of the map, every deed you have done is gathered and sealed in Light. The Moment of Triumph enshrines not the worlds you crossed — but the Guardian you became.

- Gear: The Whole Saga — Resurrection to triumph, Cosmodrome to the Dreaming City. The road is yours now. Eyes up, Guardian.
- Triumph: Moments of Triumph — Every great deed, every world walked, every god unmade — recorded for as long as the City stands.
- Triumph: A Legend Sealed — Wear the mark of the journey’s end. You did not just survive the story. You finished it.
