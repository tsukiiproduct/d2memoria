## The Guardian
- Role: The player — a Risen warrior wielding the Light
- Affiliation: The Last City / The Vanguard
- Became important: D1 · 2014 — A Guardian Rises
- Status: Alive (and hard to keep dead)

Resurrected by a Ghost centuries after the Collapse with no memory of a past life, the Guardian rises from a forgotten corpse in the Cosmodrome to become the single most consequential figure of the age — slayer of Crota, Atheon, Oryx, Ghaul, and ultimately the Witness itself. Every chapter of this archive is, in the end, their story.

## The Ghost
- Role: Companion AI and resurrection engine, a shard of the Traveler's Light
- Affiliation: Bonded to the Guardian
- Became important: D1 · 2014 — A Guardian Rises
- Status: Alive

A fragment of the Traveler given will and voice, the Ghost chose one corpse out of countless and made it a Guardian. Navigator, hacker, conscience, and lifeline, the Ghost is the reason the Guardian can die a thousand deaths and still answer every call.

## Commander Zavala
- Role: Titan Vanguard, military commander of the Last City
- Affiliation: The Vanguard
- Became important: D1 · 2014 — The Last City
- Status: Alive

The steady hand on the wall. A Titan of unbending resolve, Zavala carries the weight of the City's defense and the grief of every Guardian he has outlived. Where others chase legend, he holds the line.

## Ikora Rey
- Role: Warlock Vanguard, master of the hidden and the arcane
- Affiliation: The Vanguard / The Hidden
- Became important: D1 · 2014 — The Last City
- Status: Alive

Once a disciple of Osiris, Ikora runs the City's network of spies and seekers. Brilliant and quietly furious, she answers questions no one else dares to ask — and acts on the answers.

## Cayde-6
- Role: Hunter Vanguard, gambler and gunslinger
- Affiliation: The Vanguard
- Became important: D1 · 2014 — The Last City
- Status: Dead (D2 · 2018 — killed by Uldren Sov)

The Exo who made the Tower laugh. Cayde's death at the Prison of Elders is the spark of Forsaken and the hardest loss the Vanguard ever took. He leaves behind the Ace of Spades — and a hole nothing fills.

## Mara Sov
- Role: Queen of the Awoken, schemer beyond death
- Affiliation: The Reef / The Awoken
- Became important: D1 · 2015 — House of Wolves
- Status: Alive (in a fashion)

Sovereign of the Reef and the most patient mind in the system. Mara plays a game measured in centuries — against the Hive gods, against the Darkness, and against fate itself — and is almost never showing her real hand.

## Uldren Sov / The Crow
- Role: Prince of the Reef; later reborn as the Guardian "Crow"
- Affiliation: The Awoken; later the Vanguard
- Became important: D2 · 2018 — Forsaken
- Status: Alive (resurrected with no memory of Uldren)

Mara's brother and blade. Driven to madness hunting his lost sister, Uldren murders Cayde-6 and is killed in turn by the Guardian — only to be resurrected as the Crow, a new Guardian haunted by a past he cannot remember.

## Oryx, the Taken King
- Role: Hive god-king, the Taken King
- Affiliation: The Hive
- Became important: D1 · 2015 — The Taken King
- Status: Dead (slain aboard the Dreadnaught)

The first and greatest of the Hive kings, wielder of the Taken and master of the sword-logic that says only the strong deserve to exist. He drags a continent-sized Dreadnaught into Saturn's rings to avenge his son Crota — and dies for it.

## Savathûn, the Witch Queen
- Role: Hive god of cunning, the cleverest liar in the universe
- Affiliation: The Hive (her own faction, the Lucent Brood)
- Became important: D2 · 2022 — The Witch Queen
- Status: Dead/uncertain (her schemes outlive her)

Oryx's sister, and the mind behind half the betrayals in the saga. Savathûn steals the Light, builds Ghosts of her own, and proves that even the Traveler's gift can be taken — a truth that reshapes everything Guardians believed about themselves.

## Dominus Ghaul
- Role: Cabal emperor, commander of the Red Legion
- Affiliation: The Red Legion / Cabal
- Became important: D2 · 2017 — The Red War
- Status: Dead (consumed at the moment of his ascension)

The one who did the impossible: he caged the Traveler and tore the Light from every Guardian at once. Ghaul believed the Light was a prize to be seized by the worthy. The Traveler disagreed, and unmade him with a single wave of returning dawn.

## Eramis, the Shipstealer
- Role: Fallen Kell of the House of Salvation, wielder of Stasis
- Affiliation: House of Salvation
- Became important: D2 · 2020 — Beyond Light
- Status: Frozen (defeated on Europa)

A Fallen warlord who knelt to the Darkness rather than die in the cold, Eramis is the first enemy to wield Stasis — and the reason the Guardian learns to wield it too. Her grief and rage make her one of the saga's most human villains.

## Emperor Calus
- Role: Exiled Cabal emperor, later a Disciple of the Witness
- Affiliation: The Shadow Legion / formerly the Cabal throne
- Became important: D2 · 2023 — Lightfall
- Status: Dead (his body claimed by the Witness)

The decadent god-emperor who once threw the galaxy's grandest parties becomes the Witness's first Disciple, hunting the Veil on Neptune. Calus chooses oblivion as a kind of worship — and the Final Shape as the only feast worth attending.

## The Witness
- Role: Author of the Collapse, architect of the Final Shape
- Affiliation: The Darkness (its will made singular)
- Became important: D2 · 2019 — Shadowkeep (the Pyramids arrive); fully revealed D2 · 2023 — Lightfall
- Status: Unmade (D2 · 2024 — The Final Shape)

The still, silent end of all things. A composite of the first species the Traveler ever wronged, the Witness has spent eons seeking to collapse all of reality into one final, changeless shape. It is the enemy the whole saga was always building toward — and the one every Guardian who ever lived rises together to destroy.

## Osiris
- Role: Exiled Warlock, prophet of the Vex and the Light
- Affiliation: Formerly the Vanguard; the City's most famous heretic
- Became important: D2 · 2017 — Curse of Osiris
- Status: Alive (though he loses, and later regains, his Light)

The most brilliant and most exiled Warlock in the City's history. Obsessed with the Vex and the futures they simulate, Osiris walks the Infinite Forest hunting certainty — and his pride costs him dearly across the saga before he finds his way back.

## Rasputin
- Role: Warmind — humanity's ancient, awakened super-AI defense network
- Affiliation: Humanity / the Golden Age
- Became important: D2 · 2018 — Warmind
- Status: Alive in new form (after sacrificing himself to save the Traveler)

The last great weapon of the Golden Age, sleeping under the Martian ice until the caps melt and wake him. Cold, calculating, and not always on humanity's side, Rasputin is a force unto himself — and, in the end, a defender who pays the ultimate price.

## Lord Saladin
- Role: The last of the Iron Lords, Warlord turned hero
- Affiliation: The Iron Lords / the Last City
- Became important: D1 · 2016 — Rise of Iron
- Status: Alive

The grim keeper of the Iron Lords' memory. When the SIVA plague erupts in the Plaguelands, Saladin calls the Guardian to take up a mantle the world thought lost — and to remember those who fell so the City could stand.
